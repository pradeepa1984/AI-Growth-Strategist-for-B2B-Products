  What is AI Growth Strategist?

  It's a B2B AI-powered growth intelligence platform. You give it a company website URL — it figures out everything about that company's market position and tells you
  where to grow, what to write about, who to target, and who your competitors are. No manual research needed.

  ---
  High Level — The Big Idea

  Sales and marketing teams today juggle 5–6 disconnected tools:
  - One tool to understand their positioning
  - Another for SEO keywords
  - Another for content ideas
  - Another for finding leads

  This system replaces all of that with a single automated pipeline:

  Company Website URL
         ↓
  Website Intelligence  →  understand what the company does
         ↓
  Human Approval        →  verify the AI got it right
         ↓
  Market Intelligence   →  generate growth strategy
         ↓
  Lead Discovery        →  find real companies to sell to
         ↓
  UI Dashboard          →  display everything cleanly

  The core insight: if you can accurately understand a company from its website, everything downstream (content, SEO, leads, strategy) can be automated.

  ---
  Low Level — What Each Module Actually Does

  1. Website Intelligence

  - Takes a URL → calls Firecrawl to scrape and convert the website into clean markdown
  - Sends that markdown to Claude (Haiku via AWS Bedrock) with a structured prompt
  - Claude extracts: company summary, industry, services, ICP (who they sell to), keywords
  - Computes a confidence score — if too low, it crawls more pages and retries (iterative crawling)
  - Result is stored in DynamoDB (structured) and S3 (raw markdown)
  - A human reviews and approves the result before it moves forward — this is the human-in-the-loop gate

  2. Market Intelligence (the module you've been building)

  - Requires approved Website Intelligence as input — won't run without it
  - Makes two LLM calls to Claude:
    a. Generates: keyword clusters, content topics, target segments, top competitors
    b. Generates: multi-scale market expansion strategy (global + India-specific cities)
  - The strategy has 3 tiers — Large Scale (enterprise), Mid Scale (regional), Small Scale (niche)
  - A best fit scale is deterministically inferred from ICP keywords
  - Schema validation with one retry ensures the output is always structurally correct

  3. Lead Discovery (planned/partial)

  - Uses the ICP, industry, and services from Website Intelligence
  - Queries Apollo.io to find real companies that match the profile
  - Returns a list of potential leads to target

  What makes it different from a simple ChatGPT wrapper

  Three things:

  1. Grounded in real data — Claude only sees content scraped from the actual company website, so it can't hallucinate company facts. The intelligence is always anchored
  to what the company actually says about itself.
  2. Human-in-the-loop gate — Market Intelligence won't run on unapproved data. A human must verify the Website Intelligence output first. This prevents
  garbage-in-garbage-out.
  3. Caching layer — Once a company is analysed and approved, results are stored in DynamoDB. Re-running the same URL doesn't trigger a new crawl or LLM call unless
  forced — saves cost and time.
